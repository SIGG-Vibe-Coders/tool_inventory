How saving works

This page edits a working copy in your browser. Nothing is saved until you download the file and upload it to GitHub. Your changes become a commit, so everything stays reviewable and reversible.

  1. Make your changes below (add, edit, or delete tools).
  2. Click Download tools.json.
  3. In the GitHub repo, open data/tools.json, choose Upload / replace file, and commit. (See the README for screenshots.)
